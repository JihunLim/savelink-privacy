Account & Data Deletion

App: SaveLink · Developer: Jihun Lim

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

Delete in the app (recommended)

  1. Open the SaveLink app.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Tap Delete Account (계정 삭제) at the bottom.
  4. Confirm — your account and all related data are deleted immediately.

You can also delete from the web (savelink-ai.vercel.app) via Profile → Delete Account.

If you can't access the app

Email us from the address you signed up with and we'll process the deletion within 30 days: jihunlim204@gmail.com

What is deleted

  • Account info (email, display name, user ID)
  • All saved links and their titles, descriptions, notes, AI summaries, and tags
  • Categories and public/private settings
  • Credit earn/spend history
  • Follows/subscriptions and report/block records

Retention

Your account and the data above are permanently deleted immediately from the operational database upon request. Any residual copies in backups are fully purged within 30 days. No data is retained for any other purpose.
